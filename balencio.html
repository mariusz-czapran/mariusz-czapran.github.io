Balencio

Simulation Entertainment • Google Play Store Release

Balencio is a lighthearted wealth simulation and banking experience application built to test interface design compliance and deployment ecosystems. The primary goal was to handle an end-to-end development life cycle, managing everything from initial mockups to closed testing tracks.

To comply with production standards, I coordinated a closed alpha testing program requiring structured deployment workflows and multi-language store localizations. The application features crisp, minimalist balance dashboards that mimic premium high-end banking environments.
