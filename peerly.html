Peerly (v1.1.0)

Networking Application • Secure Mesh Local Utility

Peerly is a lightweight local application designed for direct communication completely independent of central server architectures. Version 1.1.0 focused heavily on refining internal stability parameters, squashing discovery phase bugs, and building smarter, more reliable chat synchronization logic.

The product design adheres to a strictly ad-free, data-minimalist philosophy. By bypassing massive third-party databases, it addresses direct local infrastructure capabilities while protecting communication pathways on local mesh clusters.
